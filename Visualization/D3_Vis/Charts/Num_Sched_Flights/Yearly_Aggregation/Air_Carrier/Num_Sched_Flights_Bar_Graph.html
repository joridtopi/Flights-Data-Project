Description:
This chart displays a bar graph of the yearly number of scheduled flights for a selected air carrier.

Guide:
Select an air carrier from the drop down list. The list contains every air carrier that has a recorded domestic flight between the years 1988 and 2019. Click the "Get Chart" button to update the graph.

Features:
Hover over any bar in the chart to obtain more details about that observation.
